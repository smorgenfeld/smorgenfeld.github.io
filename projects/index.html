PROJECTS
Transmission Contest
Our team's submission for our ME14 transmission contest. We designed and constructed a 6:1 planetary gearbox during April-May 2019.
Markov Masher
A Javascript utility that creates a Markov Chain using the source texts of two different Wikipedia pages. This, in effect, combines the two Wikipedia pages' content. For more on Markov Chains see the Wikipedia page. Written during late 2016 and August 2017.
EconSim
A simple economic simulation used to evaluate the impact that different tax schemes (sales tax and income tax) have on an economy. Written in Python in Late 2019, for Market Design (Ec131).
Is Tomorrow Ditch Day?
A simple webpage for determining whether tomorrow will be Caltech's ditch day. Probablities are assigned based on the past 15 or so ditch day dates. Written in Javascript/CSS in May 2018; currently updated with my prediction for Ditch Day 2020.
