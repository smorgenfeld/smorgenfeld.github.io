PROJECTS
Markov Masher
A Javascript utility that creates a Markov Chain using the source texts of two different Wikipedia pages. This, in effect, combines the two Wikipedia pages' content. For more on Markov Chains see the Wikipedia page. Written during late 2016 and August 2017.
Transmission Contest
Our team's submission for our ME14 transmission contest. We designed and constructed a 6:1 planetary gearbox during April-May 2019.
Mace Swinger
A simple Javascript text-based role-playing game built with the goal of randomly generating as much content as possible. This is the sequel; the original was written in Python. Written during late 2016.
Fallout 4 Weapon Builder
A utility for 'building' some of the weapons found in the game Fallout 4. I attempted to emulate as closely as possible the game's UI using CSS and Javascript. Built during the summer of 2016.
Is Tomorrow Ditch Day?
A simple webpage for determining whether tomorrow will be Caltech's ditch day. Probablities are assigned based on the past 15 or so ditch day dates. Written in Javascript/CSS in May 2018.
Quote of the Day Today
A webpage displaying daily quotes from Star Wars. Unfortunently, the page is currently out of quotes; I'll have to find more. Built with Javascript/CSS in October 2017.
